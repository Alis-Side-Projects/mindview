Part A: Knowledge & Understanding [15 marks]
Question 1 [3 marks]
Solve: \( 5^{x-2} = 125 \).
Solution:
\( 125 = 5^3 \), so \( x - 2 = 3 \), \( x = 5 \).

Question 5 [3 marks]
A radioactive substance has half-life 10 days. From 240 mg, find the mass after 30 days.
Solution:
30 days = 3 half-lives. \( 240 \cdot (1/2)^3 = 30 \) mg.
