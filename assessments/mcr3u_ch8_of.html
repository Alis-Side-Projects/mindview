Question 5 [2 marks]
For \( y = 5\sin(x) - 3 \), the maximum value is:
Solution:
Max = -3 + 5 = 2.
Question 6 [2 marks]
If \( y = \sin(x) \) is shifted 60° to the left, the new equation is:
Solution:
\( y = \sin(x - d) \) shifts right by \( d \). Left 60° → \( d = -60° \), so \( y = \sin(x + 60°) \).
