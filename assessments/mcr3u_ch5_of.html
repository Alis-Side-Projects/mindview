Part A: Knowledge & Understanding [15 marks]
Question 1 [2 marks]
An arithmetic sequence has \( a = 8, d = -3 \). Find \( t_{10} \).
Solution:
\( t_{10} = 8 + 9(-3) = 8 - 27 = -19 \).
Question 2 [2 marks]
Find \( t_5 \) for the geometric sequence with \( a = 4, r = -2 \).
Solution:
\( t_5 = 4 \cdot (-2)^4 = 4 \cdot 16 = 64 \).
